Instructions

How to use this standalone dashboard.

Guide
  1. Enter raw records in the Data Sheet.
  2. Use the one-column QITP hierarchy selector: Inspection Type → Subsection → open the Sub-subsection dropdown, tick the items you want, then click outside. Checked sub-subsections appear as rows; click Inspection Details below the project name to open the detail popup table.
  3. The Rating Sheet calculates actual KPI values, scores, weighted averages, and status.
  4. The Monthly KPI Data sheet stores real monthly evidence such as concrete test results, NCRs opened/closed, accumulated NCRs, and ITP compliance.
  5. The Project Analytics and Dashboard tabs turn the monthly data into trend tables, cumulative NCR balances, and presentation charts.
  6. The Summary sheet shows KPI calculation and judgement only.
  7. Use the data records as evidence for audit and management review.
  8. Zoom control: hold the right mouse button and scroll the middle mouse wheel up to zoom in or down to zoom out. Release the right mouse button to return to normal scrolling/context-menu behavior.
  9. Pan control: press and hold the middle mouse wheel, then drag to pan across wide tables, charts, or the dashboard page. Release the wheel to stop panning.
